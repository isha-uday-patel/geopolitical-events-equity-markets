# Impact of Geopolitical Events on Share Prices

## Overview
BBA research project analyzing how 12 major geopolitical events affected global equity markets, using Event Study Methodology and Paired-Sample T-Tests across 5 years of market data.

## Methodology
- Event Study Methodology
- Paired-Sample T-Test
- Short-term (±3 days), medium-term (±15 days), and long-term (±30 days) market reaction analysis

## Key Findings
- Geopolitical shocks trigger significant short-term volatility
- Long-term market recovery depends on event duration and structural impact
- Developed markets recover faster than emerging markets

## Tools
Microsoft Excel — Event Study Methodology, Paired-Sample T-Test

## Context
Conducted as part of BBA Winter Internship Report, evaluated by Veer Narmad South Gujarat University (VNSGU)
